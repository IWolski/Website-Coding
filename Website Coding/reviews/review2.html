WEBSITE REVIEW #2 - www.hongkiat.com/blog/built-with-css/

HongKiat is a website that appears to be centered around sharing articles in various areas, from development to tech and more.

Questions

  1. Does the site's home page validate?
  2. Find any other page on the site. Does the second page validate?
  3. Document how many validation errors and/or warnings may exist on each of the two pages you validate.
  4. How would you rate the site's design, on a scale of 1(horrible) to 10 (fantastic)? Why?
  5. How would you rate the site's usability on a scale of 1 (horrible) to 10 (fantastic)? Why?
  6. Does the site meet its purpose?

Answers

  1. Yes, the website validated.
  2. Yes, the second site also validated.
  3. No validation errors occured.
  4. I'd rate the site's design a 7. Overall, it's nice, but some of the pages look squished together and cluttered. Text is occasionally hard to read as well, particularly the light gray text on white background and white text on gray background.
  5. I'd rate the usability of the site a 6. To start with, the homepage is just a bunch of links to articles. The dropdowns are also links to a handful of articles, but you have to click on the navigation options or hover over 'all' to easily see most of the content. There's a search bar, but without the obvious and clear understanding of the site's intent, it's hard to guess what I should be searching for.
  6. After clicking through a few pages, it seems like the site is mainly focused on article sharing. It does technically, then, meet it's purpose. I believe it could do much better, however.
